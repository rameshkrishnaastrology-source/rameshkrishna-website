About Sri Ramesh Krishna

With over 30 years of combined family lineage in traditional Vedic Astrology, Sri Ramesh Krishna brings ancient wisdom to modern seekers. Trained in the lineage of Gurukula astrology, he combines classical methodologies with compassionate guidance to illuminate the divine path of his devotees.

His expertise spans horoscope analysis, Kundli matching guidance, Muhurtham calculations, and Prashna consultations. Each session is tailored to address your unique spiritual needs, offering pariharam (remedial measures) and dharmic guidance rooted in Tamil Nadu's traditional practices.

Sri Ramesh Krishna believes astrology is not mere prediction, but a tool for self-awareness and spiritual evolution. He has helped hundreds of families find clarity, harmony, and divine blessings through authentic astrological consultation.
